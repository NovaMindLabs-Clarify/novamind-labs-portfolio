About

One studio, two kinds of problem

NovaMind Labs is a one-person studio that treats a $600 landing page and a role-based booking platform as the same kind of problem: understand the business, cut anything that doesn't earn its place, ship something that works on the first day and holds up on the hundredth.

The templates in this portfolio exist because most local businesses are over-served by agencies and under-served by templates. The platforms exist because some of those same businesses eventually outgrow a landing page — ChairTime is what that upgrade looks like in practice.
