Our Story

From six tables to a Riverside destination

Ember & Salt opened ten years ago as a six-table room with one wood-fired oven and a menu written in chalk. The idea was simple: cook what's in season over real fire, and don't apologize for a short menu.

A decade later, the oven is still the center of the kitchen, the menu is still short, and it still changes with the season — but the dining room grew, a private dining space was added, and the kitchen team now writes a journal about what they're cooking and why.

What hasn't changed: every dish still starts with what's good that week, not what's easy to keep in stock.
