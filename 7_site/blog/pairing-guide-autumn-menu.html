A Pairing Guide to Our Autumn Menu

October 9, 2026 · Bar Team

We get asked constantly what to drink with the wood-fired half chicken, so here's the short answer: the Riverside Sour if you want something bright to cut the char, the house red if you'd rather lean into the smoke.

The ribeye is more forgiving than people expect — the bone marrow butter is rich enough that it can stand up to almost anything on the by-the-glass list, but the Ember Old Fashioned's smoked bourbon actually echoes the char on the meat rather than fighting it.

For the risotto, skip the reds entirely — the truffle gets lost under tannin. A textured white does more work here than people assume, and it's usually the better order with the mushroom dishes across the whole menu.
