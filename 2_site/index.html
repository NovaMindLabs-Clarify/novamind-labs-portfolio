Our Story

A shop built on old-school standards

Blackwood opened in 2015 with one chair and a simple idea: treat every cut like it matters. A decade later, we're still sharpening our own razors and training every barber on the floor.

No rush jobs, no shortcuts — just clean lines, honest conversation, and a shop that feels like it's yours.
