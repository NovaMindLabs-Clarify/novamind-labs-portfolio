Our Story

Flowers arranged with real care, not a conveyor belt

Petal & Stem started in 2018 with a single flower stand and a promise: every bouquet leaves the studio looking exactly as good as the ones in our photos.

We work with local growers where possible, cut and arrange every order the same day it ships, and never let a tired stem out the door.
