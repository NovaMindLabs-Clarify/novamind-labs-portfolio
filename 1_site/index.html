Our Story

Coffee that starts with a relationship, not a bag of beans

Copper Cup started in 2012 as a single roaster in a garage. Today we work directly with farms across three continents, roast in small batches every morning, and pour every cup by hand.

No shortcuts, no syrupy blends — just honest coffee, baked goods made from scratch, and a space that feels like your second living room.
